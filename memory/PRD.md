# SWRLZ-CORE Product Direction

SWRLZ-CORE is a private, local-first automation, learning, and teaching platform. Android is the first node. Future nodes include PC, server/VM, Raspberry Pi, creative-production tools, and carefully bounded physical-device adapters.

The current 0.1.5 patch focuses on:

- filter-aware allowlist controls,
- device/app/screen context,
- privacy redaction,
- editable Mentor settings,
- local-first skills,
- graceful offline operation,
- and stronger mission watchdog behavior.

See `SWRLZ_CORE_MASTER_ROADMAP.md` for the complete detailed roadmap and item descriptions.
